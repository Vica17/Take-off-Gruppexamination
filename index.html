About Us

Our idea for the company Takeoff started back in 2005 when we encountered a great need for start-ups to get started with their websites. We are located in a building from the 17th century in the beautiful surroundings of Gamla stan, Stockholm.Our team has 4 frontend developers devoted to aid start-up companies to reach out on the web. Our mission is to help companies to become successful in the digital world. We do that by taking the time to understand your goals and your business plans, and by collaborating with you to design, deliver and support high-performing web solutions that bring you significant business advantage.To empower your brand and to attract visitors to your website.
